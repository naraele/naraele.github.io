The types of essays

Essay has 5 types!!!

  1. Descriptive - Paint a Mental Picture.
  2. Expository - Explain Something.
  3. Narrative - Tell a Story.
  4. Persuasive - Convince the Audience of Your Point of View (POV)
  5. Argumentative - Explore a Topic and Establish a Point of View.
